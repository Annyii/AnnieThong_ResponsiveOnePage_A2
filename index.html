Olabukoo

MARKET

All of Olabukoo's idea has come straight from scratch and is fully out of olabukko's mind which makes this art their version of a market, specifically located in China, Hong Kong but doesn't exist. Since Olakuboo missed his hometown and family there, they wanted to make a their own version of a china based market so they could share the artwork with their family. In the artwork, the people that are visible are the poeple that mean the most to olabukoo.

Phono Forest

MOUNTAIN TOP NO.2

This piece done by PhonoForest is a day in a life of a mountain town that is going through a bright foggy day. This art expresses a lot of different emotions when exploring it with your eyes. It gives emotions of newness, nostalgia, and warmth. Back in 2014 PhonoForest said they wanted always wanted to make different versions of mountains with houses and this is their second one out of the four they made.
